### INTRODUCTION
Due to work pressure and modern-day daily activities made human lives more fast paced and stressful. People are suffering from many psychological disorders due to stress. Often stress leads to suicides and crimes. If we identify stress at early stage we can cure psychological diseases and avoid suicides and we can save lives. People are extensively using their social media accounts to share their feelings to friends, relatives and close relations. By using social media data, we can easily identify the feelings and stress of the respective person.
